We're a small team of university students who got tired of confusing customs rules. So we built something better — a friendly, AI-powered biosecurity checker anyone can use before they pack.

Our mission: make Australian biosecurity rules accessible, clear, and stress-free for every traveller.

How it all started

It started with a bag of Vietnamese snacks and a very stressful moment at Tullamarine Airport. One of our team members had their favourite treats confiscated because they didn't know about Australia's strict biosecurity laws - and BICON, the official tool, couldn't help with multi-ingredient foods.

That frustration sparked an idea. What if there was a smarter tool that could handle complex, real-world food items? Something that combined official biosecurity data with AI analysis and community reports?

That experience inspired us to create Phobidden Import. We believe that biosecurity compliance shouldn’t be confusing. With the right tools, travellers can protect Australia’s ecosystem while avoiding stressful moments at the airport.
